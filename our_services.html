While App Science tells us a lot about the interests of a user, Geo-Story allows us to see how—and where—a user chooses to spend his or her time.

Take our own CRO, Aziz, as an example. Aziz visits Whole Foods three times per week, hits a gym about once a month, and can be found in a major city airport at least six times in a given month. What does this data tell us about Aziz? He watches what he eats and likes to prepare his own food, but perhaps doesn’t exercise as much he’d like because he’s a busy man, constantly traveling for work. These behaviors make up his Geo-Story. We’ve already mapped millions of POIs to our platform, including retailers, restaurants, venues, and even events, to ensure that we create comprehensive user profiles.
